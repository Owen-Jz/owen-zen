How dense should the Life Command Center be?

Bento Grid shows everything — but HOW compact is the question

A

Glance Mode

Maximum density. Each section is a tight card — numbers, sparklines, and tiny indicators only. Think: mission control. You see everything at once without scrolling.

B

Balanced

Medium density — each card shows key stats and a small chart or visual. Some cards larger than others. A few sections scroll internally for more detail.

C

Deep Dive Mode

Generous sizing. Each section has room to show charts, mini-tables, and richer context. Fewer items visible at once but much more readable.

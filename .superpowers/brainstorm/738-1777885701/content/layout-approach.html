How should the Life Command Center be organized?

This will aggregate ALL sections into one unified view. Pick the spatial philosophy that fits how you think.

A

Bento Grid

Japanese lunchbox style — asymmetric grid of cards, each section a self-contained module. Dense but breathable. Customizable sizes.

B

Hero + Rows

One massive "hero" KPI panel at top (the numbers that matter most), then rows of section cards below, each scrollable within its container.

C

Masonry Flow

Pinterest-style variable-height columns. Sections arrange themselves naturally. More organic, harder to predict layout but more interesting visually.

D

Side Ribbon

Left sidebar acts as a live ticker/timeline of what's happening now. Main area cycles between focused deep-dives per section.

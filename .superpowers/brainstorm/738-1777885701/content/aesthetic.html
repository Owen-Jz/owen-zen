What visual personality for the Life Command Center?

The look and feel should match how you want to FEEL when you look at it

A

Dark Ops

Deep dark background, subtle glows, neon accent highlights. Think: elite hacker dashboard, data looks serious and alive. Red or amber primary accents.

B

Zen Minimal

Calm, muted palette. Lots of whitespace. Soft shadows, elegant typography. A space that calms you while keeping you informed. Monochromatic with one accent.

C

Editorial Clarity

Light background, strong typographic hierarchy, newspaper-inspired. Data presented like a premium financial terminal. Serif + sans pair.

D

Neon Cyberpunk

Vibrant saturated neon on dark. Glowing borders, scanline textures, electric colors. Feels like you're piloting something futuristic. For bold personalities.
